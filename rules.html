Server Rules

General

  • 1. External mods/hacks/glitches/duping/scripts/NVinspect are not permitted (filters and external crosshairs are allowed).
  • 2. No alt accounts or stream sniping.
  • 3. Do not evade combat/combat log. Wait 10 minutes after PvP before logging out or entering safezone unless the fight couldn’t realistically continue.
  • 4. If you log into someone else's base or locked room unexpectedly, make a ticket and don’t touch anything—even if it’s your own base post-raid.
  • 5. Glitch movements (vortexing, meatballing, headglitching) are banned.
  • 6. Report bugs/glitches. Abusing them without reporting will be punished (includes de-rendering bases).

Safezone

  • 1. Unattended dropped items are free game. Otherwise, don’t touch what’s not yours—including vehicles.
  • 2. No safezone camping, blocking, following, or abusing protection. If you kill in self-defense entering/leaving, leave their loot.
  • 3. Unattended ungaraged vehicles may be deleted after 12 hours. Place it or lose it.

Base Building

  • 1. One main base + one stash base/FOB per player.
  • 2. Max 25 code-locked doors/hatches/containers per base.
  • 3. All areas of your base must be accessible on foot—no loot rooms via windows only.
  • 4. Build realistically—no unsupported structures or more than 2 floor kits out from a structure.
  • 5. BBP kits must not overlap in ways that block access or raid tools.
  • 6. Mesh and fence kits must be fully constructed (doors/walls/fences/gates).
  • 7. On municipal buildings, silos, or radio towers, max 2 floor kits out—don’t block player structures.
  • 8. Secure your base. Don’t share codes with untrusted players—no compensation for your mistakes.
  • Note: No base size limit, but don’t abuse it. If it gets excessive, staff will notify you.

Base Storage Limit

  • You’re limited to 20 storage kits (lockers, boxes, safes, gun walls, etc) per base, plus 5 extra per team member.

Raiding

  • 1. Alliances are allowed.
  • 2. No despawning, repairing, or modifying bases during active raids.
  • 3. Loot can only be dropped if taking the storage kit—don’t despawn unwanted loot.
  • 4. No combat logging during raids. If IRL forces you to log, say so—don’t log with combat tag.
  • 5. Don’t abuse hologram/kit placement to raid (one ladder on the floor is allowed).
  • 6. Glitch methods (fences, punching through windows, missing vehicles, floating entities) are not allowed.
  • 7. A raid is considered active for 15 minutes after the last use of explosives or raid tools.
